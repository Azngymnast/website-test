Solo Bouldering:

Solo bouldering is good for you. It teaches things like beta reading for your style/box/span, it strengthens mental fortitude by forcing one to think about self preservation through sketchy topouts and commiting moves which also necessitates one to learn how to minimally pad a problem.


The process:

Its more fun and more rewarding doing and accomplishing things the hard way. Stay focused and enjoy the process
